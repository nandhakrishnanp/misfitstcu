Welcome to our Telematic Control Unit

Our Telematic Control Unit (TCU) is a cutting-edge solution designed to revolutionize the way you manage your fleet of vehicles. Whether you operate a logistics company, a public transportation system, or a delivery service, our TCU empowers you with real-time data and control capabilities like never before.

Key Features:


  • Real-Time Vehicle Tracking: With our TCU, you can track the precise location of each vehicle in your fleet, allowing for efficient route planning and optimized operations.

  • Live Performance Data: Monitor important vehicle metrics such as speed, fuel level, engine health, and more in real time. Identify issues early and take proactive measures to prevent breakdowns and reduce maintenance costs.

  • Geofencing and Alerts: Set up custom geofences and receive instant alerts when a vehicle enters or exits designated areas. Enhance security and ensure vehicles stay within authorized zones.

  • Remote Control: Remotely disable or enable vehicle systems in emergency situations or for security reasons, ensuring complete control over your assets.

Our TCU is designed for versatility, making it compatible with a wide range of vehicles, from cars and trucks to buses and specialized equipment. It's a scalable solution that can meet the unique needs of small businesses and large enterprises alike.
